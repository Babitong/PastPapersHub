Access & Registration

Do I need to create an account to access past papers?

No, you do not need to create an account or register to access any past papers on PastPapers Hub Cameroon. All resources are completely free and accessible without any registration.

Is there a limit to how many papers I can download?

No, there are no limits on the number of papers you can download or view. You can access as many past examination papers as you need for your studies.

Are the past papers really free?

Yes, all past papers on EduPapers Cameroon are completely free. We are a non-profit educational initiative dedicated to providing equal access to educational resources for all Cameroonian students.

Can I access the website on my mobile phone?

Yes, PastPapers Cameroon is fully optimized for mobile devices. You can access all features, browse subjects, and download papers from your smartphone or tablet.

Downloading Papers

How do I download past examination papers?

To download papers: 1. Browse to the subject category you need 2. Select the specific subject 3. Choose the year and paper 4. Click the "Download" button 5. The PDF will download to your device

What file format are the papers in?

All past papers are provided in PDF format, which can be opened on any device using free software like Adobe Acrobat Reader or built-in PDF viewers on most smartphones and computers.

Can I view papers online without downloading?

Yes, you can view most papers directly in your browser by clicking the "View PDF" button. This is useful if you have limited storage space or want to quickly review a paper.

Why won't my downloaded PDF open?

If your PDF won't open, try these solutions: - Ensure you have a PDF reader installed - Check your internet connection if viewing online - Try downloading the file again - Contact support if the issue persists

Content & Subjects

What examination systems do you cover?

We cover both General Education (O-Level, A-Level, BEPC, Probatoire, Baccalaureat) and Technical Education (ITVEE, ATVEE, CAP, BT) examination systems in Cameroon.

How many subjects are available?

We currently have over 25 subjects available across all examination levels, including sciences, humanities, languages, and technical subjects.

Are the answer keys included?

We provide answer keys for most papers where available. When answer keys are provided, they are clearly marked and can be downloaded alongside the question papers.

How current are the papers?

We regularly update our database with the latest examination papers. We have papers dating back several years, with the most recent papers from the last examination sessions.

Technical Issues

The website is loading slowly. What can I do?

If the website is loading slowly, try: - Refreshing the page - Checking your internet connection - Clearing your browser cache - Trying a different browser - Contacting us if the issue persists

I found a broken link or missing paper. How do I report it?

Please use our contact form or email support@edupapers.cm to report any broken links or missing papers. Include as much detail as possible about the paper you're looking for.

Can I use the website without JavaScript?

Yes, EduPapers Cameroon works without JavaScript. All core functionality, including browsing and downloading papers, is available even with JavaScript disabled.

Is the website secure?

Yes, we prioritize security and privacy. We don't collect personal information, and all downloads are safe. We regularly update our security measures to protect our users.

Other Questions

Can I contribute past papers to your collection?

Yes! We welcome contributions from teachers, schools, and students. Please contact us at contributions@edupapers.cm with scanned copies of past papers you'd like to share.

Are you affiliated with the Cameroon government?

EduPapers Cameroon is an independent non-profit initiative. We work in partnership with educational institutions and teachers across Cameroon but are not a government agency.

How can I support EduPapers Cameroon?

You can support us by: - Sharing our website with other students - Contributing past papers - Providing feedback for improvement - Volunteering as a content verifier

Do you offer other educational resources besides past papers?

Currently, we focus exclusively on providing past examination papers. We believe in doing one thing exceptionally well to best serve Cameroonian students.
