Our Mission: Equal Access to Education

PastPapers Hub Cameroon is dedicated to providing free, high-quality educational resources to students across Cameroon.

Our Story

How we started and why we continue

Bridging the Educational Divide

Founded in 2023 by a group of Cameroonian educators and software developers, PastPapers Hub Cameroon was born out of a recognition that many students lack access to quality study materials, particularly past examination papers.

We observed that while some students in urban areas could afford expensive study guides and past papers, those in rural and underserved communities struggled to access these essential resources. This disparity created an uneven playing field in national examinations.

Our platform was created to level this playing field, ensuring every Cameroonian student, regardless of their location or economic background, can access the materials they need to succeed academically.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work

Accessibility

We believe education should be accessible to all. Our platform is completely free, requires no registration, and works on all devices.

Quality

All resources are verified by qualified Cameroonian educators to ensure accuracy and relevance to current examination standards.

Community

We work collaboratively with teachers, schools, and educational organizations across Cameroon to build our resource library.

Focus

We concentrate exclusively on providing past examination papers - doing one thing and doing it exceptionally well.
